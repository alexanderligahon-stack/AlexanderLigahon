Game Project

FULL PAGE

Get ready for CoinQuest, a fast-paced, addictive game where you dash through levels collecting shiny coins while avoiding sneaky monsters! It’s simple, but that’s what makes it so fun—you can pick it up in seconds, yet it challenges your reflexes and strategy. Every coin you grab increases your score, but one wrong move could send you back to the start. CoinQuest is perfect for anyone who loves quick challenges, testing their skills, and aiming for that high score.

I built CoinQuest from scratch using GDevelop, a super beginner-friendly platform that lets you turn ideas into games without needing complex coding knowledge. Through the process, I learned essential game development concepts like player movement, collision detection, object spawning, and scoring systems. It was amazing to see my ideas come to life on the screen and to troubleshoot challenges along the way—like figuring out how to make the monsters chase the player just enough to be fun but not impossible. This project taught me that even small steps in coding can lead to something playable and enjoyable.

CoinQuest is not yet finished, but I’m hopeful about its potential and excited to keep improving it. Creating this game showed me that game development is both creative and iterative—every playtest reveals what works and what can be made better. In the future, I plan to add new monster types, power-ups, multiple levels, sound effects, animations, and maybe even a leaderboard to make the game more engaging and competitive. CoinQuest may be simple now, but it’s just the beginning of my journey, and I can’t wait to see how far I can take it.
